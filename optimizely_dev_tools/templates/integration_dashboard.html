    What are Integrations?

    Optimizely integrates with other best-of-breed platforms to enhance your experiments.

    • Find out more in our Knowledge Base - Learn more
    • Google Analytics Integration - Learn more
    • Meet Optimizely’s Technology Partners - Learn more

    What integrations are available?

    • Analytics integrations - See the impact of Optimizely experiments on common analytics data like visit duration and bounce rate.
    • Heatmap integrations - See the impact of Optimizely experiments on visitor engagement based on where they click.
    • Audience integrations - Create custom Optimizely Audiences directly from a third-party tool, so you can target and personalize experiments. (May require some manual setup)
    • Call tracking integrations - Track phone calls as experiment goals. (May require some manual setup)
    • Productivity integrations - Go faster and farther with an Optimizely productivity integration!
